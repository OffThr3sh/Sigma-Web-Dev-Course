Different Video & Audio Categories

Videos Of Different Categories

Educational Videos

These videos aim to teach or explain specific topics, skills, or concepts. Examples include online tutorials, lectures, and educational documentaries.

Entertainment Videos

Entertainment videos are created for enjoyment, relaxation, or amusement. They include movies, TV shows, comedy sketches, music videos, and vlogs.

News and Current Affairs Videos

These videos provide information about current events, news stories, and updates. News channels, interviews, and investigative reports fall into this category.

Audios Of Different Categories

Music Audios

Melodic compositions, instrumental or vocal.

Environmental Audios

Natural sounds like rain, wind, or birds.

Electronic Audios

Synthesized or digitally generated sounds.
